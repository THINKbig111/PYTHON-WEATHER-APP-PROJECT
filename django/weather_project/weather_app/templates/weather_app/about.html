About Us

Welcome to Weather Today, your number one source for accurate and up-to-date weather information. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of weather forecasting, with an emphasis on reliability, accuracy, and user-friendliness.

Founded in 2024, Weather Today has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for providing reliable weather forecasts drove us to start this website. We now serve users all over the world and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our weather forecasts as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
